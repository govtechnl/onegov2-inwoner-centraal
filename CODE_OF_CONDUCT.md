# Code of conduct

This project adopts the
[Contributor Covenant, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our
community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body
size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic
status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity
and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming,
diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Sensitivity around the topic

This challenge concerns bereavement and loss. When discussing personas,
correspondence, or scenarios, please:

- Treat the subject matter with care. Avoid graphic detail beyond what the
  brief and source materials establish.
- Use the personas as composites, not as real people.
- Centre the perspective of the bereaved partner in design discussions.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behaviour may be reported to the challenge
organisers at [hack@govtechnl.nl](mailto:hack@govtechnl.nl). All complaints
will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

The full text of the Contributor Covenant is available at
<https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>.
